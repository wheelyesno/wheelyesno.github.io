Random Spin Wheel

How to Use

  1. Tap the center button to spin the wheel.
  2. Wheel will rotate and stop on a random segment.
  3. An alert will show your result.

What Is This?

This is a simple spinning wheel tool to help you make decisions in a fun way. The wheel is randomized each time you tap the center. You can customize segments later!
