🎲 How to Use

  • Simple Decision Making: Tap the center button to spin the wheel and get your yes or no answer
  • Random Results: Each spin is completely random, giving you unbiased decisions
  • Perfect for: Quick decisions, settling debates, choosing between options, or just having fun
  • Mobile Friendly: Works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktop computers
  • No Registration: Start using immediately - no sign-up required

✨ What is This?

The Yes or No Decision Wheel is a fun and simple tool to help you make quick decisions. When you're stuck between two choices or need a random yes/no answer, just tap the button and let the colorful wheel decide for you! The wheel contains 6 sections with equal chances for "Yes" and "No" results.

🌟 Features

  • Equal Slices: Perfect 60-degree sections for fair results
  • Centered Text: YES/NO text perfectly centered in each slice
  • Colorful Design: Beautiful gradient colors make every spin visually appealing
  • Smooth Animation: Realistic spinning physics with gradual slowdown
  • Equal Probability: Fair 50/50 chance for yes or no results
  • Responsive Design: Looks great on all devices and screen sizes
  • Instant Results: Get your answer in just a few seconds
  • Unlimited Spins: Spin as many times as you want, completely free
